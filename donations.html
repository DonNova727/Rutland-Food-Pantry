Donations

Monetary donations may be mailed to: Rutland Food Pantry c/o St Patrick’s Church PO Box 939 Rutland, MA 01543

Food donations can be dropped off at the following locations:

  • Rutland Public Library (280 Main St)
  • First Congregational Church of Rutland (264 Main St)
  • St Patrick’s Church (258 Main St)
  • Rutland Public Safety Building
  • Spencer Savings Bank (93 Main St)
  • Lee’s Barber Shop (249 Main St)

Immediate Needs

We are currently in immediate need of the following items:

  • Canned Meats (Tuna, Chicken and Ham)
  • Cereal
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Canned Soups
  • Juice or Juice Boxes
  • Boxed Rice Products
  • Hamburger/Tuna Helper
  • Boxed or Canned Potato Products
  • Macaroni and Cheese

We only accept food that is not past its expiration date - as we cannot give out expired food.
