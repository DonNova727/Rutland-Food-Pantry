We Aim To Help Those In Need

Food Pickup Times

Food pickups happen on the 2nd Thursday of every month from 6:00pm to 7:00pm, and the 4th Saturday from 9:00am to 12:00pm.

Please call us at 1 (508) 866-2206 ahead of time to ensure that there hasn't been a schedule change prior to your arrival.

About Us

We are the Rutland Food Pantry. Our goal is to service those in greatest need in our community by providing food to families and support.
